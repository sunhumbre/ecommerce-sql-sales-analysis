Executive Summary

This analysis evaluated 1M+ ecommerce records to identify revenue drivers, profitability trends, and operational risks.

Major Findings

Books is the dominant revenue and profit category

Graphic Novels show elevated return risk

18–24 demographic drives majority revenue

Express shipping strongly correlates with higher revenue

Discount levels remain moderate and not margin-destructive

Business Impact

The insights enable data-driven decisions across:

Pricing strategy

Fulfillment optimization

Marketing targeting

Inventory allocation
